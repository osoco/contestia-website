How does it work?

Launch

The Widget focuses on providing a fully customizable form that seamlessly integrates with your existing systems. This widget allows you to personalize every aspect of your communication, from branding to content, ensuring that your audience receives tailored messages that resonate. With flexible integration options, the widget adapts to your specific needs, enhancing user engagement right from the start.

  • Fully Customizable Form

    This feature allows complete control over the form's layout, fields, and aesthetics, ensuring it aligns perfectly with your brand identity.

    Whether you need to collect specific information or cater to a particular audience, you can tailor the form to suit any requirement.

    This flexibility helps in creating a user-friendly interface that can lead to higher completion rates and more accurate data collection.

  • Tailored Integration

    The form is seamlessly connected with your existing platforms and workflows.

    This integration is designed to be hassle-free, supporting a wide range of APIs and services.

    This coherence between systems reduces the learning curve for your team and minimizes disruption, leading to a smoother operation and enhanced user experience.

  • Personalized Communications

    This feature leverages user data to customize each interaction, from the language and tone of messages to the timing and channel of delivery.

    By tailoring communications to the individual preferences and behaviors of users, your messages are more likely to resonate, increasing engagement and fostering stronger relationships.

    This personal touch can significantly enhance customer satisfaction and loyalty.

COLLECT

Our backoffice system streamlines your workflow with automation, enabling efficient data collection and management. It's designed to be data-driven, providing you with actionable insights to make informed decisions. Plus, with the upcoming AI Tag Generator feature, you'll be able to categorize and analyze data more intelligently, taking your operations to the next level.

  • Automated Workflows

    Fully customizable phases that allows you to design and implement workflows that perfectly fit the unique processes of contest creation and evaluator management.

    This feature enables you to automate repetitive tasks, route submissions to the appropriate evaluators automatically, and manage deadlines and notifications effortlessly.

    By reducing manual input and potential errors, these workflows ensure that your contests and evaluations are conducted smoothly and efficiently, allowing your team to focus on higher-value activities.

  • Data-Driven

    This capability involves collecting, analyzing, and utilizing data to make informed decisions and improve outcomes. With comprehensive analytics tools, you can track performance metrics, user engagement, and operational efficiency.

    These insights allow you to identify trends, predict outcomes, and adjust strategies in real-time, thereby optimizing the effectiveness of your contests and ensuring that your decisions are backed by solid data.

  • Coming Soon – AI Tag Generator

    The upcoming AI Tag Generator is an innovative feature set to revolutionize how data is categorized and utilized in Contestia.

    This tool will use AI to automatically generate tags for data entries, significantly speeding up the organization and retrieval of information.

    By leveraging machine learning algorithms, the AI Tag Generator will provide accurate, context-aware tagging, making data easier to manage and analyze.

EVALUATE

Evaapp is a powerful tool for matching applications with the right evaluators. It’s driven by data to provide observability into the evaluation process, ensuring that the best candidates are selected. With deep insights at your fingertips, you can make decisions with confidence and precision, enhancing the overall effectiveness of your evaluations.

  • Successful Application Match-Making

    This feature uses a sophisticated algorithm to assess the compatibility of candidates with specific criteria or opportunities, enhancing the accuracy of matches.

    It considers various factors, including applicant qualifications, past experiences, and specific requirements of the opportunity, to ensure a high success rate.

    This targeted approach not only improves the efficiency of the selection process but also increases satisfaction among both applicants and evaluators by aligning expectations and capabilities.

  • Data-Driven Observability

    Real-time monitoring and management enables you to track evaluator’s activity, and assess the overall performance of the match-making process. By integrating data from various touchpoints, Evaapp offers a holistic view of operations, identifying bottlenecks and opportunities for improvement.

  • Get Insights

    Extracting valuable feedback from the grades and comments provided by evaluators is crucial on each application.

    This feature analyzes evaluator feedback to give you a comprehensive understanding of an application's strengths and weaknesses as perceived by the evaluators.
